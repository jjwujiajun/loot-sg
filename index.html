What's Loot

WHO

Loot is a startup funded by NUS Enterprise, funded by alumni of the NUS Overseas College in Silicon Valley.

WHAT

Loot is a shopping platform that gives shoppers huge instant discounts across major US retailers, often at 4-30% off their shopping carts!

HOW

Our closed beta offers 21% off Forever21.com sitewide. The discount applies to all items including sale items. It STACKS with coupons too!

How Can I Order with Loot?

Browse on forever21.com USA. Send us details of the items you want to buy using the Loot form.

Loot sends you an invoice with the final price including doorstep delivery to your Singapore address. Upfront final pricing - no surprise charges!

Pay securely using credit/debit card.

We deliver your Loot within 10-15 working days anywhere in Singapore.
